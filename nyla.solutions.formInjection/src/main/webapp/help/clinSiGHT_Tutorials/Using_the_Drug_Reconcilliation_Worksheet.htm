Using the Drug Reconciliation Worksheet (DRW)

 

 

Use the following steps to complete and submit a Drug Reconciliation Worksheet (DRW).

 

  1. Begin at the SMN home page.
     

  1. Navigate to the Site pane.
     

  2. Click the Protocol/Site link to display the site specific page.
     

  3. In the left Navigation bar click Drug Reconciliation.

 

The Drug Reconciliation page displays.  Previously saved DRWs can be retrieved from the list of worksheets.  Click the Description to open a saved DRW.

 

  1. To create a new DRW, click the Create New Drug Reconciliation Worksheet link.
     

At the top of the page are the following pre-populated fields: Preparer, Prepared on, Protocol/Site, and Investigator.

 

  1. The DRW has the following sub-sections:

1 - Description

Enter a description of the drug for your reference, for example "Period A" or "Open Label."

 2 - Supplies Received/Transferred

Enter information for each drug shipment received at the site.

 3 - Total Containers

Enter information for the containers you will reconcile, for example 10 kits x 20 containers (bottles)/Kit = 200 containers.

4 - CSR / Inventory Log Summary

Enter information for each drug reconciliation / return you complete.

 5 - General Comments

Enter any comments needed to explain drug reconciliation issues.

 

  1. Complete the form.
     

  1. You can:

    • Click Save to save all information entered into the worksheet.

    • After final drug reconciliation is complete for the Period or Batch recorded in the DRW, click Submit to send the DRW to ESF.
