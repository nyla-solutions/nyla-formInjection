Scheduling a Potential Site Assessment (PSA)

 

 

Use the following steps to schedule a Potential Site Assessment (PSA).

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. Click the Potential Site Identification tab.

 

  1. Search for an Investigator.
     

Note:

Search by entering one field or multiple fields to narrow your search.

 

  1. Click Search.
     

Note:

If the search yields multiple investigators, click the button beside the name to select the investigator.

 

  1. Click Schedule Assessment Visit at the bottom of the screen to schedule a PSA Visit.

 

  1. Use the Calendar pop-up to schedule the PSA for a specific date.
     

  2. Click Submit to schedule the visit.
     

Note:

 

Don't forget - you can download the Pre-Visit Letter from the confirmation window.
