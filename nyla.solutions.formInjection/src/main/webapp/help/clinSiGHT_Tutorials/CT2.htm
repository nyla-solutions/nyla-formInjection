Posting an Issue

 

 

 

Issue management allows you to record site Issues and submit them to other study team members for your review and for resolution.

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. To post a new issue, click Post Issue at the Issue panel.
     

Note:

A Post Issue pop-up window displays.

 

  1. At the Post Issue window, complete the following required fields:

Site

Click the drop-down arrow  identified as Site to make a site selection.

Classification

Click the drop-down arrow identified as Classification to classify the issue.

Priority

Click the drop-down arrow identified as Priority to identify the level of priority.  This level determines the time-frame required for resolution of the issue.

Description

Type a description to explain the current issue.

Note:

Resolvers and Reviewers - Search for the person you want to select as resolver or reviewer.

 

Resolver

Identify a resolver and click their name in search results. Click the arrow to move the name into the resolver box.  If you make a mistake or wish to clear the Resolver box, click Clear.  The resolver has responsibility for the resolution of the issue.

Reviewers

Identify a reviewer or reviewers and click their name in search results. Click the arrow to move the name into the Reviewers box.

 

Note:

Required fields are preceded by a red asterisk.

 

  1. You can:

  • Click Cancel to close the Issue window with out posting an issue.

  • Click Save to keep the Issue to be updated later.  Save will not post the issue and no other study teams will be able to issue view it.

  • Click Submit to post the Issue, this sends the issue to the resolver and any selected reviewers.  The timeframe for the required resolutions starts when you click Submit.
