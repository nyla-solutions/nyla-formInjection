Completing and Updating the On-Site Investigator File Form (OSIFF)

 

 

Use the following steps to complete and update an OSIFF. The OSIFF allows you to track regulatory items at the site. The OSIFF is maintained in clinSIGHT and can be saved and updated throughout the course of a study.

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. From the Site Panel click the Protocol/Site.
     

Note:

On the left of the screen is a navigation bar with site-related forms.  

 

  1. Click On-Site Investigator File.
     

Note:

The On-Site Investigator File form (OSIFF) displays.

 

 

  1. Review the On Site Investigator File Form sub-sections:

Regulatory Documentation

Tracks regulatory documents, such as the 1572.

Staff

Tracks site staff involved in the study including their curriculum vitae and medical licenses.

Protocol Documents

Tracks required protocol documents which should be at the site, i.e. protocols, amendments, administrative letters.

IRB / IEC / Institution Committee

Tracks IRB information as well as IRB approvals of many documents, such as the informed consent and annual reviews.

SAEs

Tracks SAE forms completed by the site.

Lab Normals / Certification / Site

Tracks laboratory information, including accreditation information.

Staff  Training

Tracks the training of all site staff.

EDC

Tracks EDC training records and the EDC technology survey.

Other correspondence

Tracks other important documents, such as Blanket Correction Forms and the Study Closure Letter.

 

  1. Complete the form.
     

    Note:

    Enter data in appropriate fields to track documents available in the on-site investigator file.  

 

 

  1. Click Add to add additional rows as needed.
     

  2. You can:

    • Click Save to save information entered.  The saved OSIFF can be retrieved at subsequent monitoring visits.

    • Click Done to mark any item for which tracking is complete and you no longer wish to see display in the OSIFF.

    • Click Reconcile to remove all information marked as Done from OSIFF.
