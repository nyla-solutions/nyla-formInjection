Navigating clinSIGHT

 

 

Use the following steps to navigate the clinSIGHT application.

 

  1. To personalize your Homepage, go to the upper left side and identify the drop-down box identified as Roles.
     

  1. Click the drop down arrow and select your role.
     

Note:

The Homepage is now personalized for your role.

 

  1. Review the top of the homepage to locate clinSIGHT Tabs:

  • Home

  • Site Monitoring Plans

  • Potential Site Identification

  • My Forms

  • Reports
     

  1. Beneath the tabs, are a set of blue Panels, common titles of panels are:

    • Sites

    • Scheduled Visits

    • Issues

    • Alerts

    • Staff
       

  1. Beneath each panel are Elements.
     

Note:

 

At the Issues panel, you will view elements related to the issues available to you, such as Protocol/Site, Priority, Classification and Description, etc..

 

  1. You can:

  • Filter panel results via the drop down arrow in the panel heading.

  • Sort your results via any underlined element headings.

  • Maximize your panels by clicking on the upper right corner box.

  • Obtain additional detail information by clicking underlined data within a panel.  For example, clicking a specific Protocol/Site will display a page containing site detail information.  A tab will be created in the Tab section of the Homepage specifically for the site.

 

Note:

You can always return to the Home page by clicking the Home Tab.
