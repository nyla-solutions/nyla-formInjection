Documenting a Significant Ad hoc Site Contact (SASC)

 

 

 

Significant Ad hoc Site Contacts include telephone and email communications with sites which should be documented and submitted to Electronic Study File (ESF).  You can complete a Significant Ad hoc Site Contact form (SASCF) for any site, even if you do not have an IMPACT assignment for the site.

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. Click on the My Forms tab pane.
     

  1. At the left navigation panel select the Significant Ad hoc Site Contact (SASC).
     

  2. At the pop-up window select a Protocol / Site from the drop down.
     

  3. Click Submit.
     

Note:

The SASCF opens and the communication can be documented.

 

  1. Click on Submit to complete the Significant Ad hoc Site Contact.

 

Note:

This prompts you to apply your electronic signature and sends the report to ESF.  SASCs do not go to a reviewer, they are submitted directly to ESF.

 

You can also access the SASCF from the left navigation bar at a site page.  This opens a SASCF for the sites for which you have an IMPACT assignment.
