Completing the Site Monitor Visit Form (SMVF)

 

 

Any visit conducted on-site during the Manage Study phase should be documented on an SMVF.  Additionally phone monitoring assessments are also recorded on the SMVF during Manage Study.

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. Navigate to the Scheduled Visits panel.
     

  2. Click on Visit Type link to open the form available for this scheduled visit.
     

  1. Click on the SMV Link to open a Site Monitor Visit Form (SMVF) for the scheduled site visit.
     

Note:

The Site Monitor Visit Form (SMVF) displays.  Note at the top of the page are pre-populated fields: Preparer, Prepared on, Protocol/Site, and Study Status.

 

  1. Click on the Visit Type drop down to select the visit type for this visit.

 

Note:

Some sections of the form will default to "N/E" depending on the visit type.

 

 

  1. Review the Site Monitor Visit Form (SMVF) sub-sections:  

1 - General

Records the general site visit information, including who was present at the visit.

2 - Investigator Staff and Form Staff

Records changes in facilities or staff available at the site.

 3 - Protocol Compliance

Records compliance with study compliance and procedures.

 4 - Documentation

Records items related to source documents and case report forms.

 5 - Clinical Supplies

Records information related to study drug / investigational  product.

 6 - Others

Records other important information reviewed during the monitoring visit.

 7 - SAE

Records SAE information reviewed during the monitoring visit.

8 - Subject Information

Records source data verification completed during the monitoring visit.

 

 9 - Additional Comments

Records general comments related to the monitoring visit.

 

 

  1. You can:

    • Click Save any time you want to save the information recorded.

    • Click Submit to send the report to the Site Manager or Protocol Manager for review.

    • Navigate through the form by clicking the links at the top of the form.

    • Add rows for additional data.

    • Delete rows you have previously entered.

    • Post an Issue that will be linked with this monitoring visit report.
