Documenting a Phone Monitoring Visit

 

 

Some studies may allow for monitoring visits done by telephone.  The Protocol Manager will provide specifics about these studies in the Site Monitoring Plan.  If you need to complete a Phone Monitoring Visit, begin by scheduling an SMV for the site.

 

  1. Login to the Homepage.
     

    Note:

     

    You must be online to complete a phone monitoring visit.

 

  1. Navigate to the Scheduled Visits panel.
     

  2.  Click Visit Type link to select the Site Monitor Visit Form (SMVF) for the identified site's scheduled SMV.

 

  1.  Locate the Visit/Assessment Type drop-down box, select Phone.
     

  2. Many sections of the SMVF will default to "N/E."  In the remaining sections document the phone monitoring assessment.

Protocol Compliance

Document information related to the site's compliance with study requirements.

Specify

Click on the Specify link to open a comments field in which to provide a response to an interview question in the guidelines.  

Comments

Add a comment to document additional information obtained during the phone monitoring visit.

 

  1. Document all relevant information for the site assessment.  When finished, click Submit to apply your electronic signature and submit the report for review.
     

 

  1. From the top of the Site Monitor Visit form:

    • Click Save to save information entered.

    • Click Delete to remove all the data entered in the form.

    • Click Protocol Deviation to open a Protocol Deviation form.

    • Click Post Issue to record an issue that will be linked with this monitoring visit.
