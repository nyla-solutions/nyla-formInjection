Completing a Site Monitoring Plan (SMP)

 

 

Use the following steps to complete a Site Monitoring Plan (SMP).

 

  1. Click the Site Monitoring Plan tab.
     

  1. At the left side is the Navigation bar, click the Site Monitoring Plan List link.  This will access the list of site monitoring plans which you have created or have been assigned to review.

 

  1. To create a new site monitoring plan, click the Site Monitoring Plan Link this will open a blank site monitoring plan for you to complete.
     

  2. Review the Site Monitor Plan sub-sections:
     

Note:

Note the Preparer is pre-populated at the top of the page.

 

 1 - General

Information about the study and specific details that enable the Site Monitor to monitor the study according to the plan.

2 - Pre-Study Evaluation Visits

Provides the Site Monitor specific details required to perform the Pre-Study Evaluation Visit and provide a more realistic assessment of a potential Site's capabilities.

3 - Site Initiation Visits

Provide the Site Monitor with specific details required when performing the Site Initiation Visit.

4 - Site Monitoring Visits

Provides the Site Monitor with specific details required when performing routine monitoring visits during the course of the study.  This section includes structured interview guidance for phone monitoring visits.

5 - Site Closure Visit

Provides the Site Monitor with specific details required for conducting the Site Closure Visit.

6 - Review

Allows you to select the reviewers for your SMP.

 

  1. Complete the form:
     

    Note:

     

    The SMP allows the completion of the form in stages.  Each section can be completed separately if needed.  Each section completed after the first will be saved in ESF as a new version.

 

  1. You can:

    • Click Save to save information entered and complete the SMP or section at a later time.

    • Click Submit to send this to your selected reviewers.

    • If changes need to be made to an SMP:

      • Access the form from the SMP tab.

      • Click Edit to open the form.

      • Make any changes.

      • Click Submit to complete the SMP and file it in ESF.
