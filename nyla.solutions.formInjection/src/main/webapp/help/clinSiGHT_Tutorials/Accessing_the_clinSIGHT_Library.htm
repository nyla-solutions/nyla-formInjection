Accessing the clinSIGHT Library

 

 

Use the following steps to access the clinSIGHT library.  The Library contains study and site-related documents and reports.  This information is retrieved from other systems and is available two days prior to scheduled monitoring visits.

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. Click the Protocol/Site you want to view at the Site panel.

 

Note:

The Site Overview page displays, a navigation bar at the left of the screen contains links to the Site Overview, Library, Letter templates and Monitoring Forms.

 

  1. Click Library.
     

Note:

The Library for the specific Protocol/Site is displayed.  In the main panel you can click on the Category field to filter the documents by categories, or you can define a date range to limit the results you see on the screen.

 

  1. Click the title link to review the document.

 

  1. When finished reviewing the close document.

 

Note:

 

The Library is available offline, access the Library by following the steps listed above while in the offline environment.
