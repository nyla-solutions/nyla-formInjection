Send a Broadcast Alert

 

 

Use the following steps to create and send a Broadcast Alert.  Only Protocol Managers (PTM) and Operations Leads (OPL) can send Broadcast Alerts.

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. From the Alerts panel heading, click on Create.

 

Note:

An Alert window displays

 

  1. Click the drop-down arrow identified as Protocol to select the protocol for the Alert.
     

  2. Click on Protocol.
     

  1. In the Send to box, select a role that the Alert will be sent to.
     

  2. Click the right arrow button to move the selection into the right side of the box.
     

Note:

 

Repeat for additional roles.

 

  1. Click in the Description box and enter the Alert description.
     

  1. You can:

• Click Cancel to cancel the Alert and not send it.

• Click Submit to send the Alert to the selected recipients.
