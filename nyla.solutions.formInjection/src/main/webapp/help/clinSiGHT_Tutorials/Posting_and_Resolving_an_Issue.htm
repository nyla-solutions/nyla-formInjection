Reviewing Alerts

 

 

 

Use the following steps to respond to review Alerts.  

 

  1. Begin at your Homepage.
     

  1. Go to the Alert panel.

 

  1. Locate an alert in the Alert panel.
     

  2. Click the subject alert link.
     

Note:

 

An Alert pop-up window displays, view the alert, noting Protocol/Site, Alert Created, Severity, and Description:

 

  1. You can:

  • Click Retain to keep the alert

  • Click Remove to discard the alert
